ABOUT THE GAME

Super Mario Bros is the iconic platform game developed and published by Nintendo for the Nintendo Entertainment System (NES). First released in 1985, this game revolutionized the gaming industry and became one of the most influential video games of all time.

In this adventure, you play as Mario (or Luigi in 2-player mode), navigating through the Mushroom Kingdom to rescue Princess Toadstool from the antagonist Bowser, the King of the Koopas.

The game consists of eight worlds with four levels each. Mario must reach the flagpole at the end of each level to progress. Along the way, you'll collect coins, discover power-ups hidden in "?" blocks, and defeat enemies by jumping on them.

GAME CONTROLS

MOVEMENT

  • → Move right
  • ← Move left

ACTIONS

  • B/Shift Run/Throw fireballs
  • A/Ctrl Jump
  • Start/Enter Pause game
  • Select/Space Select options

POWER-UPS

SUPER MUSHROOM

Makes Mario grow and allows him to break bricks

FIRE FLOWER

Allows Mario to throw fireballs at enemies

SUPER STAR

Makes Mario temporarily invincible to enemies

1-UP MUSHROOM

Gives Mario an extra life
